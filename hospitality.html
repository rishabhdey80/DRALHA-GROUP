Hospitality

Hotel Dralha is a certified 3-star hospitality property that combines contemporary comfort with Bhutanese hospitality traditions. Strategically located in Thimphu, the capital city of Bhutan, the hotel caters to both international tourists and local guests.

The hotel features 32 well-designed guest rooms, including 12 fully equipped apartment-style accommodations for long-term stays with modern amenities and fully equipped kitchens.

Hotel Dralha offers authentic Indian cuisine led by an experienced chef from India. Additional facilities include a conference room for business events and a rooftop bar with panoramic views of Thimphu city.
