# Dralha Group – Company Profile

## Overview
Dralha Group is a dynamic Bhutanese business group with a diversified portfolio spanning the food production, hospitality, and manufacturing sectors. Established in 1988, with its registered office located in the Industrial Estate in Phuentsholing, Bhutan, the Group has consistently demonstrated a strong commitment to quality, innovation, and ethical business practices.

Over the past decades, Dralha Group has built a reputation for reliability and excellence through the development of sustainable and impactful businesses that contribute to Bhutan’s economic growth. As part of our continued expansion, we are currently seeking financial support to enhance our operational capabilities, including the relocation and modernization of existing industrial facilities, as well as funding for a new sustainable venture, Dralha Bamboo Revolution.

Bhutan’s economy continues to grow steadily, driven largely by the Government’s investment in hydroelectric power generation and the rapid development of the country’s tourism sector. The nation’s rich cultural heritage, combined with the Government’s strong commitment to environmental conservation and preservation of traditional values, has created significant opportunities for the establishment of eco-friendly industries and agricultural manufacturing projects.

In alignment with the vision of His Majesty the King and the national philosophy of Gross National Happiness (GNH), Dralha Group has strategically focused on investing in environmentally sustainable and agriculturally driven industries. As part of this commitment, the Group has identified the bamboo industry as a key area of investment due to its strong commercial potential and its environmentally sustainable nature.

In addition, the modernization and expansion of our existing Flour Mill and Napkin Factory will further strengthen our contribution to Bhutan’s manufacturing sector while supporting national sustainability objectives. Both the proposed and existing industries are expected to generate valuable foreign exchange earnings through exports to India and other regional markets.

## Subsidiaries

### 1. Dralha Flour Mill
- Established: 1988
- Location: Industrial Estate, Phuentsholing, Bhutan

Dralha Flour Mill, established in 1988, stands as the pioneering flour mill in Bhutan, setting an exemplary standard within the country’s flour production industry. As the only flour mill operating in the Kingdom, the company has played a critical role in fulfilling domestic demand for high-quality flour products.

Located in the Phuentsholing Industrial Estate, the facility initially operated with an installed production capacity of 60 tons per day (TPD). In 2010, the mill underwent a major modernization and capacity expansion, increasing its production capability to 100 TPD.

The milling process at Dralha Flour Mill begins with the meticulous cleaning and tempering of wheat kernels, followed by controlled grinding using specialized roller systems that split the kernels open. The resulting particles, known as break flours, are then carefully sieved and processed before final packaging.

#### Core products
- Atta (Whole Wheat Flour)
- Maida (Refined Flour)
- Suji (Semolina)
- Wheat Bran

These products have gained strong market acceptance due to their competitive pricing, consistent quality, and reliability. Raw materials are sourced from key agricultural regions in Bihar and West Bengal in India, as well as through procurement partnerships with the National Cooperative Export Ltd in New Delhi.

In addition to serving the domestic Bhutanese market, Dralha Flour Mill exports products to India and Bangladesh, strengthening the company’s regional trade presence.

#### Future Plans
- Expansion into wheat-based cookies and biscuits
- Production of healthy cereal products
- Establishment of a bread manufacturing unit
- Development of a noodle production facility

Dralha Flour Mill has built significant goodwill both domestically and internationally through its commitment to food safety, quality control, and consistent supply. As Bhutan’s sole flour milling operation, the company remains dedicated to maintaining and enhancing product quality while meeting the country’s growing demand.

A key competitive advantage for Dralha Flour Mill lies in Bhutan’s low electricity tariffs, which are among the most competitive in South Asia, allowing the company to maintain cost efficiency and strengthen its position in export markets.

### 2. Hotel Dralha
- Established: 2018
- Location: Olakha, Thimphu, Bhutan

Hotel Dralha is a certified 3-star hospitality property that combines contemporary comfort with Bhutanese hospitality traditions. Strategically located in Thimphu, the capital city of Bhutan, the hotel caters to both international tourists and local guests, providing a welcoming and comfortable environment for business and leisure travelers alike.

Since its establishment, Hotel Dralha has developed a strong reputation as a reliable and high-quality accommodation option in the capital city.

#### Accommodations
The hotel features 32 well-designed guest rooms, each thoughtfully arranged to provide a comfortable and relaxing stay. Among these units, 12 are fully equipped apartment-style accommodations, designed specifically for long-term guests or visitors seeking an extended stay experience.

These apartment units include modern amenities and fully equipped kitchens, offering guests the convenience and comfort of a home-away-from-home environment.

#### Dining Experience
Hotel Dralha offers a high-quality culinary experience through its authentic Indian restaurant. The restaurant is led by an experienced chef from India and presents a diverse menu highlighting the rich flavors and traditions of Indian cuisine.

Guests can enjoy both formal dining experiences and casual meals, making the restaurant a popular choice for hotel guests and local patrons alike.

#### Business and Events
To serve business travelers and event organizers, Hotel Dralha provides a fully equipped conference facility suitable for corporate meetings, workshops, and small professional events. The conference room is fitted with modern equipment and comfortable seating to ensure efficient and successful business gatherings.

#### Rooftop Bar

This stylish space provides an ideal setting for guests to relax and socialize after a day of sightseeing or business meetings. Guests can enjoy a selection of cocktails, beverages, and light refreshments while taking in the scenic views of the capital.

#### Commitment to Excellence

### 3. Dralha Napkin Factory
- Established: 2009
- Location: Industrial Estate, Phuentsholing, Bhutan

Dralha Napkin Factory was established in 2009 as a small cottage industry specializing in the production of tissue and hygiene paper products. Over time, the factory has expanded its operations and product range to meet the growing domestic demand.

The factory currently produces:
- Two varieties of table napkins
- Two varieties of toilet paper rolls
- Facial tissues

The facility currently operates within the premises of the Dralha Flour Mill, allowing the business to leverage shared infrastructure and operational efficiencies.

Dralha Napkin Factory supplies products to a wide customer base including:
- Hotels and resorts
- Retail shops and convenience stores
- Supermarkets and commercial establishments across Bhutan

The company’s products are competitively priced and tailored to meet the needs of Bhutan’s expanding tourism and hospitality sectors.

#### Future Plans
- Increasing overall production capacity
- Expanding and diversifying the product range
- Acquiring new machinery for facial tissue production
- Installing kitchen roll production machinery
- Introducing specialized equipment for M-Fold hard paper and paper towel products for hospitals and clinical laboratories

Through these improvements, Dralha Napkin Factory aims to strengthen its position in Bhutan’s growing consumer goods and hygiene product market.

### 4. Dralha Bamboo Revolution (Under Development)
- Project Stage: Under Development
- Location: Dam Dum Industrial Estate, Samtse, Bhutan

Dralha Bamboo Revolution is a new and ambitious project focused on utilizing Bhutan’s abundant bamboo resources alongside bamboo sourced from neighboring Indian states such as West Bengal and Assam.

The project aims to establish a modern bamboo processing facility capable of producing a wide range of bamboo-based industrial and consumer products. The initiative is expected to play a significant role in promoting sustainable manufacturing, supporting rural livelihoods, and enhancing Bhutan’s eco-friendly industrial sector.

The project aligns strongly with Bhutan’s national environmental priorities and represents a key step toward building a sustainable and export-oriented bamboo industry in the region.
