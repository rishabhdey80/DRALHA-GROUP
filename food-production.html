Dralha Flour Mill, established in 1988, stands as the pioneering flour mill in Bhutan, setting an exemplary standard within the country's flour production industry. As the only flour mill operating in the Kingdom, the company has played a critical role in fulfilling domestic demand for high-quality flour products.

Core Products

Atta (Whole Wheat Flour), Maida (Refined Flour), Suji (Semolina), Wheat Bran

Export Markets

Serving Bhutan, India, and Bangladesh with reliable supply chain

Future Plans

Wheat-based cookies, healthy cereals, bread unit, noodle production

Competitive Edge

Bhutan’s low electricity tariffs enable cost efficiency for exports
