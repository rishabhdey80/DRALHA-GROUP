Achievements and Milestones

Dralha Group has a proud history of growth and achievement spanning over three decades. From our pioneering beginnings to our ambitious future plans, we continue to set new standards in Bhutanese industry.

1988

Establishment of Dralha Flour Mill - Becoming Bhutan's first flour mill.

2009

Launch of Dralha Napkin Factory to meet growing domestic demand.

2010

Modernization of the flour mill - Expansion from 60 MT to 100 MT per day capacity.

2015

Introduction of toilet paper production at the Napkin Factory.

2016

Machinery upgrade - Replacement of older napkin production equipment reducing production time.

2018

Opening of Hotel Dralha achieving 3-star certification.

2019

Retail packaging unit established for Atta and Maida (900g, 1kg, 5kg packs).

2020

New toilet roll machine installation improving efficiency.

2021

Hotel Dralha expansion - Added 12 new units, rooftop bar, conference facility.

2026-2027

Future: Bamboo Processing Plant completion marking major sustainability milestone.
